Stephan Talking jQ - Translator API:



eg. the numbers translator:

	$.stephanTalking.translate.numbers(text); //returns modified text

	example:
	var out_text= $.stephanTalking.translate.numbers(in_text);

	where 'in_text' might be "123" and 'out_text' would be " won tU thrE "




New Translators can be added on, just follow the pattern of jq_stephanTalking_t-numbers.js
